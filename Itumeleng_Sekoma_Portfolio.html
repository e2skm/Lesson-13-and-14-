Project 2: Technical Services System

This is a project I completed with a team of colleagues in 2023 for our Work Intergrated Learning module, at ICEP.

In this project myself and a team of ICEP interns from different disciplines within the ICT Faculty worked together to develop the Technical Services System (TSS). Our objective was to develop a system that will allow TUT Staff members to log a report on any issues that requires a Technician/ Artisan to resolve. Whether it is a broken aircon, blocked toilet or broken door TSS assisted TUT staff members report these incidents with ease. Previously Staff members had to call the Technical Services Department to report such incidents and this was time consuming to add to the distress incidents would take years before they got resolved. We proposed and developed TSS for TUT and during our evaluation the Lecturers were exicited to hear about the system. I played the role of a Business Analyst and Scrum Master. I learned a lot about the Agile Scrum Methodology and gained valuable work experience.
